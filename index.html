About Lab

DCN Networking Research Laboratory was established in Fall 2003. Our objective is to make significant contributions to optical, wireless, gigabit networks and Network relative fault tolerant embedded system design . The laboratory was headed by Dr. Chuan-Ching Sue, Dept. of Computer Science and Information Engineering,National Cheng Kung University. If you are interested in knowing more about us, please browse our DCNLab Web page, or contact us at suecc@mail.ncku.edu.tw
